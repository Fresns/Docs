Delete Account

Config functions according to the relevant data legal regulations of the country where the target market is located.

Delete Buffer Day
Users can revoke the deletion (restore the account) within the buffer days, and the deletion process will be executed if the expiration is not revoked.
Logical Deletion: Only mark the account as deleted in the database, and do not really delete the data.
Physical Deletion：Real data deletion, user profiles and published content will be deleted for real.
